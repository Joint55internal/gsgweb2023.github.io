Goverment of Ukraine statement on Poverty and Developement issues:

5 June 2023

A statement from Goverment of Ukraine on developement issues in Ukraine Amidst Russian Invasion

==================================================

From: Press and Information Office

Published 5 June 2023

===================================================

Goverment of Ukraine said during press conferenes:

"Despite the challenges posed by Russian invasion, Ukraine remain steadfast in its commitment to address poverty and promoting sustainable development. However, impact of the invasion on our economy, social fabric , as well on our peple is significant."

"To mitigate the effects of poverty and foster development, the government of Ukraine has taken several crucial steps, including the implementation of comprehensive social welfare programs that provide direct assistance to those affected by the conflict. These programs include targeted financial support, access to healthcare services, and educational opportunities for displaced individuals."

"Ukraine is also actively engaging with international partners to seek financial and technical assistance to support our poverty alleviation and development efforts. We welcome collaboration with organisation such as United nations, NGOs, and other nations committed to promoting global prosperity and stability."

"The government of Ukraine is fully committed to tackling poverty and advancing development, even in the face of external aggression. We are determined to restore stability, rebuild our nation, and provide a better future for our citizens."
