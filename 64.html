Published 4 June 2023

As the Press and Information Office of the Government of Ukraine, we are delighted to announce the launch of our website, designed to provide you with up-to-date information and resources. Our website is now live and serves as a comprehensive platform to facilitate effective communication and engagement among all delegates. We invite you to explore its various features and make use of the available resources.

We believe that our website will serve as a valuable resource, fostering greater collaboration and understanding among the delegates. We encourage you to share this announcement with your fellow delegates and make the most of the available features. If you have any suggestions, feedback, or encounter any issues while navigating the website, please do not hesitate to reach out to us. Your input is invaluable in helping us continually improve our services.

Thank you for your attention, and we look forward to engaging with you on the website and during the upcoming GSG.

Website: https://joint55internal.github.io/gsgweb2023.github.io/

Regards

Press and Information Office of the Government of Ukraine
